We're happy to announce that the BMFTR has funded the DEMOS project as part of the ErUM-Data Call. The overall project is coordinated by Prof. Mikhail Mikhasenko at the University of Bochum. We will contribute jointly with Philipp Eller from Elisa Resconi's research group.
